Sandwich Recipe
Ingredients:-
1.Dish, Spoon 2.Bread 3.Chooped Vegetables 4.Spieces 5.Mayoneese
Recipe
1.Take chopped vegetables and mix it mayonese. 2.Pour some amount spieces. 3.Pour it in dish and mix it very nicely with spoon. 4.Take some amount of matter and put it in bread. 5.Close the bread by putting other bread on it's top. 6.Your sandwich is ready
